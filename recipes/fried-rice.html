Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This easy Fried Rice recipe is something you won't forget easily.

It is easy and you only need a couple of ingredients

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Tomato
  • Peas
  • Carrot
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Prep your rice and wash it
  2. Cut the vegetables
  3. Fry all together
  4. Serve!
