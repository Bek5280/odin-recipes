Description

This easy Burger recipe is something you won't forget easily.

It is easy and you only need a couple of ingredients

Ingredients

  • Beef (or choice of meat)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Avocado
  • Bun
  • Choice of Sauce

Steps

  1. Prep your beef
  2. Cut the vegetables
  3. Grill the burger
  4. Arrange burger
  5. Serve!
